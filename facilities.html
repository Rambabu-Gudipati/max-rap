  • IIT MADRAS

    • 1. Thin Layer Chromatography

      • The IATROSCAN MK-6s is combined with Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) and a Flame Ionization Detector (FID) for these separation and quantification of organic compounds.

      • Chromarod, a quartz rod coated with a thin layer of silica or alumina, is used to separate the sample components via chromatography.

      • The instrument offers quantitative analysis of the separated organic components, enabling the SARA (Saturates, Aromatics, Resins, Asphaltenes) analysis of bitumen.
